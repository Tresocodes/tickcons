About Tick Construction Ltd

Our Story

Tick Construction Ltd is a forward-thinking and dynamic construction company, officially registered in May 2025. Although new on paper, our company is founded on the seasoned experience of four professional engineers, each bringing a wealth of hands-on expertise in building design, site management, and civil infrastructure. We are committed to delivering exceptional construction solutions that are grounded in technical excellence, professional integrity, and client-centered service.
